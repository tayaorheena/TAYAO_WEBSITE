About Me


I'm Rheena. My life has been shaped by a constant balancing act between responsibility and personal desire. Growing up, I often felt like I had to be the family’s rock, stepping in to care for my younger siblings and manage household expectations while silently navigating my own struggles.

As the eldest child, I’ve always felt a mix of responsibility and freedom shaping my journey. My love for online gaming provided an escape, allowing me to connect with friends while navigating life’s ups and downs. Traveling has also been a passion, enriching my life with diverse experiences and memories with my family.

During the pandemic, when everything changed, I stepped up by managing my own online shop. It was challenging yet rewarding, teaching me resilience and entrepreneurship. I also gained valuable experience working as a kitchen server, where I learned the importance of teamwork and hard work.

Now, as I embark on my first year of college, I’m excited to blend my interests with my studies. My family and my partner in life are my greatest support, encouraging me to pursue my dreams while cherishing our time together. This journey continues to shape who I am, balancing my passions with the love and responsibility I hold dear.
